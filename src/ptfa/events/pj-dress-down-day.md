A pyjama dress down day in exchange for a donation of a luxury Christmas hamper item for the PTFA's Christmas hamper raffle, which will be held on 15 December.

Suggested items we would like:

* Christmas puddings
* Specialty teas/coffees/soft drinks/mixers
* Christmas cakes
* Shortbread
* Mince pies
* Chocolates
* Sweets
* Jam/chutneys
* Crackers (for cheese)

Please note:
* No nuts, or homemade food items
* Please ensure that any perishable items **will not have passed their use by/best before dates at the time of the raffle draw (15 December 2025)**
* Alcoholic items must be handed in to the school office
